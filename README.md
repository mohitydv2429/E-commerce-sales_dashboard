## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and profitability using **SQL** for data extraction and **Power BI** for dynamic data visualization. The interactive dashboard helps stakeholders track key performance indicators (KPIs), identify top-performing regions, understand customer payment preferences, and analyze monthly profit trends to make data-driven decisions.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
Based on the data analyzed in the dashboard, the key business milestones achieved are:
* **Total Amount (Revenue):** 438K
* **Total Profit:** 37K
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 5,615 units
* **Average Order Value (AOV):** 121K

## Core Business Insights & Data Analysis

### 1. Geographical Sales Performance
* **Top Performing States:** **Maharashtra** leads the market with a total sales amount of **102K**, closely followed by **Madhya Pradesh** at **87K**.
* **Emerging Markets:** Uttar Pradesh (38K) and Delhi (23K) represent steady consumer demand with high potential for targeted marketing campaigns.

### 2. Consumer Behavior & Demographics
* **Top Customers by Value:** **Harivansh** emerged as the highest-contributing customer with a purchase value of **9.9K**, followed by Madhav (9.4K) and Madan Mohan (7.8K).
* **Category Dominance:** **Clothing** is the most dominant product category, accounting for **63%** of the total quantity ordered. Electronics stands at 21% and Furniture at 17%.

### 3. Payment Mode & Transaction Analysis
* **Cash on Delivery (COD):** Remains the most preferred payment method, capturing **44%** of total transactions.
* **Digital Adoption:** UPI is strong at **21%**, while Credit Cards (12%), Debit Cards (13%), and EMI (10%) make up the remaining share, showing a balanced use of digital banking channels.

### 4. Product Sub-Category & Profit Analysis
* **High Profit Drivers:** Printers, Bookcases, and Sarees are among the top sub-categories driving maximum profit margins.
* **Seasonality & Trends:** Profit tracking by month reveals strong positive peaks in the first half of the year (Jan-Apr), with significant drops and seasonal shifts observed during August and year-end.

## Database Schema & Analytics Structure
The underlying data infrastructure consists of relational tables optimized for complex analytical querying:
* `Customers`: Contains demographic and registration details.
* `Orders`: Tracks transaction dates, order IDs, and mapping.
* `Products` & `Categories`: Manages stock profiles, item definitions, and pricing.
* `Payments`: Log of payment methods (COD, UPI, Cards) used by customers.

### Sample SQL Analysis Performed:
* YOY and Month-on-Month (MoM) revenue trend calculations.
* Customer lifetime value (CLV) and repeat purchase behavior metrics.
* Product affinity and sub-category cross-sell optimization queries.

## Tech Stack & Tools Used

* **SQL / MySQL:** Utilized for data cleaning, joining transactional tables, and generating aggregated business views.
* **Microsoft Excel:** Used for initial data sorting, quick sanity checks, and structural formatting.
